About ZHF

After years of developing a love and talent for the Culinary Arts I have recently decided to switch career paths and embark on an even more challenging journey: the Technical World of Web Development. I specialize in backend services mostly creating Databases (SQL and noSQL) as well as creating REST API's. My tech stack consits of Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap(solely for the grid system), React.js, Express.js, and jQuery. I have linked a few of my projects as well as the link to my github.
